Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

BP Companion ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application developed by Terror LLC that helps you track and monitor your blood pressure and heart rate readings. Your health data is sensitive, and we take its privacy and security very seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email address (or a private relay address) and a unique user identifier. We use this solely to create and manage your account.
  • Blood Pressure Readings: Systolic and diastolic blood pressure measurements you record in the app, including dates, times, and any notes you add.
  • Heart Rate Data: Heart rate measurements you record or sync from Apple Health.
  • Medication Information: Any medication details you choose to log within the app, such as medication names, dosages, and schedules.
  • Apple HealthKit Data: With your explicit permission, we read blood pressure and heart rate data from Apple HealthKit and may write your recorded measurements back to HealthKit. We only access the specific health data categories you authorize.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to BP Companion Premium, purchase and subscription status is managed through Apple's StoreKit in-app purchase system.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and provide app functionality
  • To store and display your blood pressure and heart rate history
  • To sync health data with Apple HealthKit when you grant permission
  • To track your medication schedule and send optional reminders
  • To generate trends, charts, and insights from your health data
  • To manage your subscription status

HealthKit Data

BP Companion integrates with Apple HealthKit to read and write blood pressure and heart rate data. Your HealthKit data is handled with the highest level of care:

  • HealthKit data is only accessed with your explicit permission
  • HealthKit data is never sold, shared with third parties for advertising, or used for purposes unrelated to providing app functionality
  • HealthKit data is stored securely and encrypted in transit
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device's Settings

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database storage for your account and health records
  • Apple HealthKit: Reading and writing blood pressure and heart rate data with your permission
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Apple Sign In: Authentication

Data Retention

Your account data, health readings, and medication information are retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app's settings, or by contacting us directly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or health data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality. Your health data is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any parties beyond what is required to operate the app.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive health data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication, encrypted database storage, and strict access controls. Health data is treated with the same level of care as protected health information.

Children's Privacy

BP Companion is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

  • You can view all data stored in your account at any time within the app
  • You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's settings
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through your device's Settings
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at: support@terrorllc.com
